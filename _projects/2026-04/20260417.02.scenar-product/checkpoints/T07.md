## Decisions Made

1. **Co-located narration**: Source of truth is `scenarios/<id>/narration/`; `public/demos/` is a build artifact
2. **Video output to CWD**: Not co-located with scenarios (MP4s are too large for git)
3. **staticFile() by default**: With `useStaticFile={false}` opt-out for non-standard setups
4. **Math.round for frame conversion**: Matches Stigmer's tested `msToFrames` function
5. **Provider nesting**: `TimeSourceProvider` wraps `VideoExportProvider` (matches Stigmer)
6. **Optional Remotion deps**: `remotion`, `@remotion/renderer`, `@remotion/bundler` are optional peer deps on the CLI
